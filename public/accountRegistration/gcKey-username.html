Create Your Username

Please keep your Username secure. For more information on how your privacy is protected, please refer to our Personal Information Collection Statement.

Your Username must contain between eight and sixteen characters, no special characters (for example: %, #, @) and may contain up to seven digits. When creating your Username, we recommend that you:

  • make your Username easy for you to remember and hard for others to guess;
  • avoid using personal information such as your name, Social Insurance Number (SIN), mailing address or email address;
  • always keep your Username secure and do not share it with anyone.
