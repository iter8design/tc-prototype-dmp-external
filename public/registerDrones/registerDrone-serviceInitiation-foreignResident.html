{First name} {Last name} Register a drone

You must register to operate any drone weighing between 250g and 25kg within Canada. Benefits of registration include:

  • If you lose your drone, registration may help in returning it to you if it is found
  • If there is a recall from the manufacturer, we may be able to notify you

There is a $5 non-refundable fee to register a drone.

Eligibility

Any Canadian citizen or permanent resident of Canada who owns a drone that weighs between 250g and 25kg.

Drones under 250g do not need to be registered. Drones over 25kg also do not need to be registered, but require a special flight operations certificate instead.

As a foreign resident of Canada, you may not register a drone in Canada. Contact us for further information.

What you need before you start

It should take 3 minutes to register a drone. To begin, you will need the following information:

  • purchase date (if applicable)
  • make, model, serial number, weight and type of drone
  • a Visa®, Mastercard®, or American Express® credit card or an Interac® Online debit card offered by a participating financial institution
    • To use Interac® Online, your bank card will have an Interac® Online logo on the card. Interac® E-Transfer and Interac® Flash may not be used for online purchases.
